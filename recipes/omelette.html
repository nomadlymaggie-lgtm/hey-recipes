Omelette

A simple omelette recipe.

Ingredients
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • filling(s) of choice, like cheese or meat

Steps
  1. In a small bowl, whisk the eggs, salt, and pepper together until lightly frothy.
  2. In an 8-inch non-stick pan, heat the butter over medium-low heat. Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to make sure they're evenly spread out, then turn the heat down to low. Cook the omelette for 2 to 3 minutes, or until the edges are cooked through and the top is slightly wet but not runny.
  3. If you'd like to add filling, add it to half of the omelette. Then use a spatula to fold the empty half on top and slide the omelette onto a plate.
